What is DAW Remote

DAW Remote is a wireless DAW(Digital Audio Workstation) software controller, especially optimized for Apple Logic®.

Instead of getting stuck in front of your computer to compose, record, and produce music/audio, you can use your iPhone/iPod with DAW Remote to control DAW functions anywhere in your studio (wirelessly). Why? Because let’s face it, wires suck!

Not only does DAW Remote work for Apple Logic, it’s also compatible with other popular DAW softwares such as Ableton Live, Cubase and other MIDI-enabled-softwares. As DAW Remote uses Apple Bonjour over WIFI network, you DON’T need to install ANY softwares or drivers in your Mac to enable it.

It's Wireless

DAW Remote use air not wire. You can use your DAW Remote everywhere without warring about wire.

It's Lightweight

DAW Remote only has simple but essential functions. It's design for you to saving your life instead of wasting time on uncountable buttons and knobs which may never been used.

All about Timeline

Your notes and emotions lie on your timeline. You need repeat and repeat playing to make sure the everything is just right. And DAW Remote is just designed to do so. JOG and Suttle mode can let you control playback very easy and efficient.
