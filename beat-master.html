About Beat Master

Beat Master is a digital tuner APP designed for our client Tactson, a company who dedicates to create and manufcture innovative musical products for the whole world musicians.

BEAT is the basic unit of time in music, no matter if you play drums, piano, guitar, or any other musical instruments, BEAT is the basic skill you have to master, it refers to the ability to "keep time" accurately in what you play, also, BEAT is a skill that have to be learned and improved by a “METRONOME”.

There are a lot of “METRONOMEs” for music leaning on the market, but if you have an IOS device such as IPHONE, IPAD, IPOD TOUCH, you are lucky to own a professional metronome that could be installed in your device. Here it is – BEAT MASTER.

BEAT MASTER is a highly accurate metronome app for musicians to learn BEAT with 64 Time Signatures and 42 Subdivisions. Here below are the details of this amazing application.

Features List

  • TEMPO the tempo range is from 30 to 240 bpm. When you set the tempo, the Italian name of the tempo range will also be displayed.
  • TAP TEMPOPress the switch to the tap mode and press TAP to find your own tempo.
  • ACCENTEach beat can be assigned as an accent on the top of the screen.
  • SOUND4 kinds of metronome sounds.
