Featured APPs

DAW Remote

DAW Remote is a wireless DAW(Digital Audio Workstation) software controller, especially optimized for Apple Logic®.

MIDI Pad

MIDI Pad is a MIDI controller. It provides one X-Y Pad, one slider and four buttons. Moreover, it can transfer your iPhone/iPod’s sensor signal (acceleration transducer and microphone) into MIDI signal.

Beat Master

There are a lot of “METRONOMEs” for music leaning on the market, but if you have an IOS device such as IPHONE, IPAD, IPOD TOUCH, you are lucky to own a professional metronome that could be installed in your device. Here it is – BEAT MASTER.

Tune Master

Tune Master turns your iPhone/iPod/iPad into a portable instrument tuner with most of the common instruments tuning modes, such as guitar, bass, violin, ukulele, Mandolin, Banjo, Dobro, Bouzouki, Dulcimer, Bagpipe and 2 Chinese traditional instruments, Guzheng and Erhu. There are some kinds of tuning modes for each instrument also, such as FLAT tuning, Drop D/Drop C tuning.
