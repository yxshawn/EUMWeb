Pro Metronome

LED flash metronome. Let you hear and see the beats.

APP Store

What is Pro Metronome

Pro Metronome is designed for both iPhone and iPad. Free version almost suits for every instrument player. It has 12 daily-used time signatures and 5 metronome tones. Thanks for RTP(Real-Time Playback) technology, it's highly accurate.

It not only has sound feedback, but has visual effects to let you SEE the beats. Upgrading to full version enables Color and Flash Mode.

Color mode could help you SEE the beats if you are playing noisy instruments such as Guitar, BASS and Drum. With extremely high-brightness LED Flash*, no matter you are in a band or in a symphony orchestra, it could help your band members sync beats easily.

It's Accurate

Pro Metronome uses our brand new RTP(Real-Time Playback) technology to ensure the high accuracy.

SEE the beats

You may find it was hard to sync your band or orchestra when you were playing. But now, everybody sees the beats.

It's Universal

You don't need purchase iPhone and iPad separately. Pro Metronome is universal APP. It's works on all of your iOS devices.

Features List

  • User-friendly. Extremely easy to use
  • 12 daily-used Time Signatures
  • 5 best-selected Metronome Tones
  • TAP to calculate BPM
  • power-saving mode , continue playing with screen-locked
  • Universal APP , support all iOS devices (iPhone/iPod and iPad)
  • RTP (Real Time Playback) technology inside, extremely accurate
  • Color Mode , let you SEE the beats. (upgrade to Full Version to enable)
  • LED/Screen Flash Mode *. (upgrade to Full Version to enable)
