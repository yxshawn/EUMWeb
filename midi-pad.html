What is MIDI Pad

MIDI Pad is a wireless MIDI controller. It provides one X-Y Pad, one slider and four buttons. Moreover, it can transfer your iPhone/iPod’s sensor signal (acceleration transducer and microphone) into MIDI signal.

Whether you are an electronic music artist, a DJ using Ableton Live to mix or remix tracks, a VJ to lighten the club , or a more traditional musician using Logic Pro on stage or in the studio, you will find the MIDI Pad to the intuitive, convenient instrument that directly links your inspiration with your computer.

Features List

  • One XY Touch Pad to control two parameters in one interface
  • Two MIDI mappable function button
  • Two MIDI mappable toggle button
  • One slider
  • Microphone signal to MIDI signal
  • Movement signal to MIDI signal
  • Use WI-FI to connect with host computer
  • No extra drivers required on host computer
  • Works with: Ableton Live, Logic, Pro Tools, Digital Performer, Cubase, Mackie Tracktion, Modul8, Motion, Reason

CC assignments

  • XY Pad axis X: CC51 axis Y: CC50
  • Buttons CC46, CC47, CC48, CC49
  • Slider CC52
  • Microphone Signal CC53
  • Acceleration Signals X: CC54 Y: CC55
