What is MIDI Pad

MIDI Pad is a MIDI controller. It provides one X-Y Pad, one slider and four buttons. Moreover, it can transfer your iPhone/iPod’s sensor signal (acceleration transducer and microphone) into MIDI signal. Whether you are an electronic music artist, a DJ using Ableton Live to mix or remix tracks, a VJ to lighten the club , or a more traditional musician using Logic Pro on stage or in the studio, you will find the MIDI Pad to the intuitive, convenient instrument that directly links your inspiration with your computer.
